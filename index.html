Welcome to Bazeman Hazeh, an adaptive halachic prayer scheduling app to meet all your needs!

Bazeman Hazeh enables users to view accurate halachic times for any location worldwide. With Bazeman Hazeh, the user can quickly and accurately view the relevant halachic and prayer times for the day. The application uses the SwiftAA Solar Package for real-time, accurate calculations. Bazeman Hazeh also supports the addition of User Created Times, which allow users to add new Halachic Times that reflect their Halachic Traditions, as well as a customizable interface, which allows users to personalize the display page. This iOS application also supports custom notifications to remind users of upcoming times. Not to mention, the app also has a Community page where you can view local businesses or fun activities in your area!

The home page is where the magic happens. Here, you can see an editable and ordered list of prayers and the exact time, down to the very second, of when each will be. Want to change your location? That's no problem. Bazeman Hazeh incorporates a city and coordinate lookup in addition to using live location. You can also change the date and view times for the next day, week, or even year!

With our easy to use settings page, you can view a list of default prayer times and choose to remove them, edit them, or even add new ones! It is easy to change the parameters for each time including the name, alarm, and dependency on hour or sun angle. Settings are automatically saved and update the home page.
